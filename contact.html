Frequently Asked Questions

How many patches are in one jar? ▼

Each jar contains 60 patches (30 pairs). Used daily, one jar lasts a full month.

How long should I leave patches on? ▼

We recommend 15-20 minutes for optimal results. You can leave them on longer (up to 30 minutes) for extra hydration, but they work best while still moist.

Can I use them on other areas? ▼

Yes! Our patches work beautifully on forehead lines, smile lines (nasolabial folds), and neck wrinkles. Anywhere you want targeted collagen treatment.

Are they suitable for sensitive skin? ▼

Absolutely. Our formula is dermatologist-tested, hypoallergenic, and free from parabens, sulfates, and artificial fragrances. We recommend a patch test if you have extremely reactive skin.

What is your return policy? ▼

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied for any reason, contact us for a full refund — no questions asked. Even if the jar is open.

Do you ship internationally? ▼

Yes, we ship to 30+ countries worldwide. US orders enjoy free standard shipping. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout.

Where are the patches manufactured? ▼

All FLORARY eye patches are manufactured in GMP-certified facilities in Seoul, South Korea — the global capital of skincare innovation.
