About This Product

FLORARY Premium Collagen Eye Patches are formulated in Seoul's leading cosmetic laboratories, combining traditional Korean beauty wisdom with modern dermatological science. Each hydrogel patch is saturated with a concentrated serum of marine collagen peptides, three molecular weights of hyaluronic acid, 24K gold nanoparticles, and botanical extracts.


The unique hydrogel delivery system ensures maximum absorption, while the ergonomic shape hugs your under-eye contour for a seamless fit. Use daily for best results — most customers notice visible improvement within 7 days.

Key Active Ingredients

  • 24K Gold Nanoparticles — Boosts circulation and delivers antioxidant protection

  • Camellia Sinensis (Green Tea) — Powerful anti-inflammatory and antioxidant

How to Use

  • Cleanse and tone your face as usual
  • Use the included spatula to lift one pair of patches
  • Apply to clean, dry under-eye area with the wider end toward the outer corner
  • Leave on for 15-20 minutes
  • Remove and gently pat remaining serum into skin
  • Use daily for best results, morning or evening

Pro Tip: Refrigerate for an extra cooling, de-puffing effect. Can also be applied to forehead lines, smile lines, or neck.
